Cooking Recipe: Cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Vanilla
  • Eggs
  • Brown Sugar
  • Oats
  • Cranberries
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Mix flour,egg,baking soda,brown sugar, in a large bowl
  2. Add in vanilla and oats, cranberries and raisins
  3. Bake to 200 degrees celsius for 7 minutes
Flour1 cup
Baking Soda1/4 cup
Vanilla1 tsp
Eggs1 egg
Brown Sugar1/2 cup
Oats1 cup
Cranberries1/2 cup
Raisins1/2 cup
